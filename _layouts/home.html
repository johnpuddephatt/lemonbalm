We believe nature is essential for wellbeing, that gardening is good for body and mind

This is the idea that drives our work, it underpins everything we do. It’s a belief that’s both rational and evidence-based, shaped by the ﬁndings of academic research as well as by our own experiences.

Gardening is good for the mind

Gardening makes you feel good. Understanding the needs of a garden helps us understand our own.

Gardening is good for the body

Our work gets people active, whatever their level of ﬁtness.

There’s more to a successful garden than just seeds, soil and water

Lemon Balm is led by professionals with qualiﬁcations in social and therapeutic horticulture, garden design, forest schools and volunteer management.

We’re trained by Occupational Therapists so our approach and outcomes are both evidence-based and rigorous.
